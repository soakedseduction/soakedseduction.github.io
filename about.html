About

How the shoots are set up.

Soaked Seduction produces underwater adult content: nudity, explicit, solo and partnered... all to be sold on adult platforms. Shoots happen in a private heated pool. This page covers the setup, how shoots are planned, and what's included in booking.

The pool

Private heated pool.

Private pool, kept at a comfortable temperature.

Address details and identifying photos of the location aren't shared publicly. Talent get the address when a date is locked in.

How shoots get set up

The work happens before the shoot day.

Underwater shoots are hard to improvise. So the concept, content level, wardrobe, rate, and timing are all worked out in advance.

Planned before shoot day

Concept, looks, references, and timing get worked out by email or DM before a date is held.

Boundaries are discussed first

Content level, wardrobe, rate, travel, and anything off limits is talked through before booking.

Paperwork and ID at booking

Model release, age verification, and required record-keeping are part of every booking.

Pool orientation before shooting

On arrival, a walk-through of how underwater shots work, signals, and the stop signs we use.

Short underwater takes with breaks

Takes are usually 10–30 seconds, with surface breaks between. Comfort checks are built into the schedule.

You can pause or stop

At any point, for any reason, without explanation. Time committed is still paid per the agreement.
