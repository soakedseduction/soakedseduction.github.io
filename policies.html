Policies

Public overview.

This is a public overview. Full shoot-specific details are discussed before booking.

Age verification

Talent must be 18+. Age is verified with government-issued ID before any shoot is booked.

ID and paperwork

Valid government-issued ID and a signed model release are required at booking, with required record-keeping completed before shooting starts.

Testing when applicable

For content where testing is industry-standard, current testing documentation is required prior to the shoot. Specifics are confirmed in writing during booking.

Payment terms

Rate and payment terms are agreed in writing before a date is held. Time committed is paid per the agreement.

Travel terms

If travel is involved, terms — including who covers what and any per-diem — are agreed in advance.

Cancellation and rescheduling

Cancellation and rescheduling terms are documented in the booking agreement, including timing windows and any deposit handling.

Professional conduct

Respectful conduct from everyone on set. Harassment or pressure of any kind ends a shoot immediately.

Safety

Pool orientation before every shoot. Short underwater takes with surface breaks. Talent can pause or stop at any time without explanation.

Privacy

Personal information collected for booking and verification is kept confidential and used only for the purposes it was collected for.

BTS and social media

Any behind-the-scenes content, on-set photos, or posts that include talent require prior agreement.

Location confidentiality

Address details and identifying photos of the location are not shared publicly. The address is given when a date is locked in.

Content scope

Soaked Seduction produces underwater adult content for commercial distribution on adult platforms (Fansly, ManyVids, OnlyFans, and similar). Shoots include nudity, explicit content, and partnered work (solo, B/G, G/G, B/G/G). Shoots do not include bondage, anything rough, or anything extreme. The specific scope for any booking — what's in, what's out, wardrobe, content level — is agreed in writing before the date is held. What gets shot on the day is exactly what was agreed, nothing more.

Questions?

Ask in your application, or send a note before applying.
