A few things to know if you've never done one before.

Heated, private pool

Underwater shots are usually 10–30 seconds at a time, with breaks at the surface in between.

Breath control matters more than swimming ability. You don't need to be a strong swimmer.

Every shot is described before it happens — pose, timing, where to look, when to surface.

Calm pace. No pressure to push past what was agreed.
